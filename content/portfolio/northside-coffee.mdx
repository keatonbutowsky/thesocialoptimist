## The challenge

A first-time founder opening a third-wave coffee bar in Plano. No following,
no email list, no shoot day on the books. Six weeks to opening.

## The strategy

- **Waitlist landing page** with daily "build-in-public" updates
- **Founder POV TikToks** — selecting beans, testing pastries, hiring the team
- **Local UGC** — 14 Dallas creators booked for opening week
- **LinkedIn for B2B catering** — a quietly profitable revenue stream nobody
  was talking about

## The result

- **3,200 waitlist signups** before doors opened
- **Line down the block** opening Saturday — covered by D Magazine
- **12,400 check-ins** in month one
- Catering pipeline filled through Q3 from a single LinkedIn launch post
