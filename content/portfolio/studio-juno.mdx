## The challenge

Studio Juno was filling classes but losing members at month four. Their social
was a calendar of class times and stock-y wellness quotes. No reason for
existing members to follow, no reason for new ones to discover them.

## The strategy

- **Member-of-the-week** spotlights (with consent and on-brand styling)
- **Owner-led carousels** on form cues, mobility tips, and the *why* behind sequences
- **TikTok** as a discovery engine for first-timers nervous to walk in
- Community engagement focused on replying inside every member&apos;s tagged stories

## The result

- **47% lift in member retention** at month six
- **+5,100 followers** across IG + TikTok in 90 days
- Class no-shows down ~20% (members feel known)
- Two new partnerships with local wellness brands sourced from DMs

> &ldquo;Our members feel seen now. That changed everything about the studio.&rdquo;
> — *Founder, Studio Juno*
