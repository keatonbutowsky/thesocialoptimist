## The challenge

Velvet Bloom had a loyal local following and a stunning catalog of work, but
their Instagram looked like a static portfolio and TikTok didn&apos;t exist.
Bookings were trending down as competitors leaned harder into short-form video.

## The strategy

We rebuilt the content engine around three pillars:

- **Process content** — time-lapses of arrangements coming together
- **Owner POV** — Megan, the founder, narrating her favorite stems of the week
- **Cultural moments** — Dallas weddings, seasonal drops, color-of-the-month edits

We shot one half-day per month and produced 20 posts plus a steady drip of
Reels. Community engagement focused on tagging local venues and replying to
every comment within four hours.

## The result

- **+312% reach** in 90 days
- **8,400 new followers** across IG and TikTok
- **22 inbound wedding inquiries** directly attributed to Reels
- Featured by two Dallas wedding publications

> on social again.&rdquo; — *Founder, Velvet Bloom Florals*
